Finch West LRT - Project Coordinator - click here

In my role as Project Coordinator for the Finch West LRT project, I took charge of the Multi-Use Pathway (MUP), overseeing various aspects of the project. This included meticulous tracking of material usage, monitoring labor hours, ensuring schedule adherence, and enforcing safety standards.

My responsibilities extended to managing concrete pouring, overseeing testing according to MTO specifications, and ensuring accurate rebar placement as per Issued for Construction (IFC) drawings.

I conducted material takeoffs and successfully led the team to complete the tunnel construction within the established schedule.

Collaborating closely with the MTO inspector, I played a vital role in addressing and rectifying comments from subcontractors. This involved maintaining open communication, facilitating inspections, and ensuring adjustments were made to meet specified standards and regulations.

Championing a project that prioritizes transportation equity, I am committed to meeting diverse community needs and ensuring strict adherence to specified standards, contributing to a more inclusive and accessible transportation infrastructure.
